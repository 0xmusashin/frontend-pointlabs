We're building at the intersection of AI and Web3, exploring new frontiers in decentralized intelligence.

## Our Mission

At Point Labs, we believe the future of artificial intelligence lies in decentralization. Traditional AI development is concentrated in the hands of a few large corporations, creating concerns about access, transparency, and control.

We're changing that narrative by building open infrastructure that enables:

- **Distributed model training** across global compute networks
- **Verifiable AI inference** using zero-knowledge proofs
- **Fair incentive mechanisms** for contributors and validators

## Why Now?

The convergence of several technological advances makes this the perfect time to build decentralized AI:

1. Matured blockchain infrastructure capable of handling complex coordination
2. Advances in cryptographic techniques like zkSNARKs and zkSTARKs
3. Growing demand for transparent and auditable AI systems
4. Community-driven development models proving their effectiveness

## What We're Building

Our initial focus is on three core areas:

### Decentralized Training Networks

We're developing protocols that allow distributed compute providers to collaborate on training large models without centralized coordination.

### Privacy-Preserving Inference

Using zero-knowledge proofs, we're building systems where users can verify AI outputs without revealing their inputs.
